---
title: "Between the rows"
date: 2026-04-01
dedication: "for the data that speaks, and the people who listen"
---
There is a silence between the rows,
where meaning waits to be named.
I have built cathedrals from columns,
mapped the distance between question and answer,
and still the data hums
with something I cannot query.

Some truths do not fit in a schema.
Some joins return more
than you asked for.

I learned this teaching fractions to children
who could not see the whole,
and again at midnight
debugging a pipeline that refused to flow.

The work is the same:
make the complex simple,
make the invisible seen,
and trust that someone on the other side
is waiting for the light to land.
